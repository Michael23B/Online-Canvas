Controls:

[Q] - makes the colour more red
[W] - makes the colour more green
[E] - makes the colour more blue
[S] - makes the brush smaller
[D] - makes the brush bigger

Controls (cont.):

[0-9] - places a happy face on the screen :)
[!!!] - clears the screen (very dangerous)
[:)] - starts a fun game (first to 50 points)

You're always losing colour if you don't draw stuff. You get more colour while drawing.
You can see what colour you're about to paint at the top right.
